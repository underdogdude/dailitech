Our Study Case - SaaS

DailiTech, we help customer design and configure infrastructure along with development pipeline, so customer’s services achieve high SLA. Beside the infrastructure, practical development pipeline is also our expertise, so we can help their development process to be more agile as well as less development lifecycle. Doing SaaS needs to handle cost structure well because the cost structure leads to customer’s offering that can set as a packages of features and functions. We implement security in every part and also work with customer that security is job zero in both infrastructure and application. And finally, we guide customers how to handle support workflow and case management.

We have practices from consulting and implementation to customers in many use cases including migration, landing zone implementation, development pipeline implementation, and also big data implementation. Our experience in FSI is security-is-job-zero mindset and adopt the segregation of duty concept.

To transform the customer to adopt with cloud solution, we normally start with a team.Customer’s team needs to understand the concept of AWS, so when we move workloads fromon-premise or commodity cloud to AWS, they understand what we suggest and how we design thesolution. After migration is complete, we help customers to optimize and innovate theirbusiness with AWS advanced technology such as AI/ML.
